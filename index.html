Getting Here

By Air

The nearest major airport is Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA), approximately 2.5 hours away

From Seattle Airport

  • Rental Car: Available at the airport. Drive to Bainbridge Island Ferry Terminal, take ferry to Bainbridge, then drive to Port Townsend (total ~2.5 hours). Or drive around through Tacoma, stop at Hama Hama, get some oysters, camp, stop at Finnriver, etc.
  • Shuttle Service: Rocket Shuttle offers direct service - advance booking required
